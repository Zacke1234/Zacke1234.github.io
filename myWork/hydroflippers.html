Introduction

On this game I worked on the character movement for both players, the boat and the surfer that trails along behind the boat.

Systems




Role: Movement programmer

Team Size: 15

Time: 4 weeks

Hydro-Flippers was our second game project at FutureGames, it is a mobile game.

The game was built with C# and Unity together with a team of 15 people assigned by FutureGames.

Hydro-Flippers

Character Controller

Getting the character movement to function along side with the water physics bobbing was quite tricky, had to consider the directions, the physics and how the designers wanted the "jumping" to work with the ramps. Alongside with the design and construction of the main levels map, had to make sure the player never would get stuck into the wall/ground etc.
